The Types of Audio Work

There are multiple was to work with audio. The two main ones are:

  • Live Audio Engineer or A1
  • Studio Engineer

The one we are going to talk about is live audio.

Live Audio

When working with live audio. It's different then working in a studio. A lot of it is practiced with a teacher before you ever get a chance to do it yourself.
When you start getting better you need to know a bit about frequencies and how to use them to fix sound right.

Frecuency Tip

Here is a frequency chart and tips on what they do when working on someones voice.




Frequency What it does
0-280 hz This is what is called low end. It is used to get rid of the bass or low rumble in a voice.
280-2k hz These are your Mids. This is where you adjust for the main body of someones voice.
2k-20k hz These are your Highs. You adjust here to get rid of percing sound and to brighten up a persons voice.
